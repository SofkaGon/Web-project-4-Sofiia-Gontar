Community and culture

Education

Education: This area includes schools and learning centers that serve the whole community. They are designed to support children and adults in gaining knowledge and skills for the future.

Cultural Spaces: There are places dedicated to celebrating Indigenous culture and history. These spaces help keep traditions alive and offer opportunities for sharing stories, art, and ceremonies.

Community Learning: This part supports programs and activities that bring people together to learn from one another and build strong community ties. It encourages respect, understanding, and growth for everyone.

Sports and Recreation

These spaces are for sports, exercise, and outdoor activities, including parks, sports fields, playgrounds, and walking trails. Having places like these helps keep the community healthy and active. For Indigenous communities, they provide areas to practice traditional games, hold gatherings, and promote wellness.
